new version 2.7
in this version I did a lot of updates and also optimizations, especially on the problem of plant and leaf movement

  • new sky reflection on water
  • new cloud reflection on the water
  • water optimization
  • new effects when it rains
  • fix fog that is too thick
  • fix the shadow bias by light source objects
  • new sky scattering
  • increased brightness of color tones and lighting
  • new reflections from light source blocks on water
  • reduce the intensity of light bursts on the sun
  • improve sun reflection on water
  • fixed plant and leaf movement issues that occurred on some devices
